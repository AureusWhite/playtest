Perk: Teacher's Pet

Description:
The "Teacher's Pet" perk characterizes your character as someone who thrives in academic environments and seeks to impress authority figures. This perk is designed for players who excel under positive reinforcement.

Effects:

    Increases XP Gains from Classroom Activities: Your character earns experience points at an enhanced rate during educational activities, reflecting their eagerness and diligence.
    Increases Reputation with School Staff: You build strong relationships with teachers and school staff, gaining favor and possibly unlocking additional support. Note that this perk does not affect interactions with other types of staff.

Gameplay Impact:
This perk is well-suited for players who excel in structured environments and seek to maximize their academic and social standing within the educational system. It focuses on leveraging positive feedback for personal growth.
