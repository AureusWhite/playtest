The Snack Area is a cozy and inviting space filled with cheerful, child-friendly decor. Brightly colored tables and chairs are set up for group snacks and meals, encouraging social interaction among rejuves. The area is stocked with a variety of healthy and tasty treats, ensuring that everyone finds something they enjoy. Large murals of fruits and vegetables decorate the walls, creating a fun and educational environment. This is a place where rejuves can refuel, relax, and enjoy some downtime with friends.
