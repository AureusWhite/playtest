Perk: Slacker

Description:
The "Slacker" perk offers a laid-back approach to life, allowing your character to navigate the game with minimal effort. This perk is designed for those who prefer to take things easy and avoid heavy responsibilities.

Effects:

    Allows Less Effort and Fewer Quests: Your character can complete fewer quests and responsibilities, reflecting a relaxed attitude toward tasks.
    Reputation Gains are Halved: While your character can maintain a low profile, their reputation gains are reduced by half. Reputation losses remain unchanged, reflecting a balanced approach to social interactions.

Gameplay Impact:
This perk is ideal for players who want to focus on personal interests and avoid the pressure of frequent responsibilities. It provides a more relaxed gameplay experience with a focus on maintaining a low-key presence.
